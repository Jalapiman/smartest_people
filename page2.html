Notable People and Their SAT Scores

The SAT is a critical way to measure intelligence especially with how important your SAT score is for the colleges you could get into, we compiled some people whose high SAT scores translated to lots of success in many other ways.


  • Mark Zuckerburg scored a perfect 1600 on the SAT and went on to attend Harvard University to study psychology. He later went on to create Facebook or Meta as its known by now, and now has built himself a company which is one of the AI tech giants of today.
  • Bill Gates had a near perfect score with a 1590 and went to Harvan university aswell, but he dropped out to pursue his comapny, and that company he dropped out of harvard for is Micosoft.
  • And lastly, Paul Allen, He hada perfect 1600 and attended Washington State University and went on to drop out also to pursue Microsoft with Bill Gates.

The SAT definetly is a gateway and a great measure for the ammount of potential someone has to be truly great, a solid way to show intelligence and help people take the "next step" in their lives.
